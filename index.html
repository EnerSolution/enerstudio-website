About EnerStudio

Built because we needed it.
Shared because you do too.

EnerStudio was built out of a real frustration. Creating professional marketing videos for multiple brands required expensive production agencies, complex software with steep learning curves, and weeks of back-and-forth. The existing AI tools either produced low-quality output, required manual work between platforms, or charged per video in a way that made regular production unaffordable.

We set out to build one platform that handles the entire production pipeline — from a text description to a finished, downloadable MP4 — without requiring any video editing knowledge, production skills, or large budget.

EnerStudio is trusted by energy companies, training organizations, real estate agencies, healthcare providers, and technology businesses across Canada and internationally. We built it for our own brands first. We are sharing it because the problem it solves is universal.
