FAQ

Common questions.

What is EnerStudio and how does it work? +
EnerStudio is an AI video production platform. You describe your video, the platform writes the script, generates cinematic AI scenes, records professional voiceover, and delivers a finished MP4 video — automatically. No video editing skills required.
What video types are available? +
Cinematic AI video and AI Avatar video are live now. Whiteboard animation and Cartoon animation are coming soon. All video types are included in every plan — you do not pay extra for different types.
How long does it take to generate a video? +
A 30-second cinematic video takes approximately 5-8 minutes from clicking Generate to download. Script generation takes 15-30 seconds. Scene image generation takes 30 seconds per scene. Video rendering takes 30-90 seconds per clip. Final stitching takes about 60 seconds.
Can I use EnerStudio for any industry? +
Yes. EnerStudio is a global platform open to any industry — solar, real estate, healthcare, education, technology, hospitality, retail, and more. Simply describe your video in your own words and the AI adapts to your industry and audience.
Do I own the videos I create? +
Yes. All videos created on paid plans are yours with full commercial rights. You can use them on social media, websites, advertising, training, or any other commercial purpose with no attribution required.
What languages are supported? +
EnerStudio supports 29 languages for voiceover generation including English, French, Spanish, Farsi, German, Portuguese, Japanese, and more. The AI script writing also supports multiple languages.
What happens if I need more videos than my plan includes? +
You can purchase additional videos at the extra video rate for your plan — $5.50 on Starter, $4.50 on Pro, and $3.50 on Business. Or upgrade to a higher plan mid-month for a prorated difference.
How does the free trial work? +
When you create your account and add a payment method, you receive one free 30-second video. This gives you the full production experience — script, scene images, voiceover, and finished MP4. You are not charged until your trial video is complete and you choose to continue.
